Online training

The PROTECT-Europe project aims to increase HPV vaccine uptake in girls and promoting gender-neutral vaccination across Europe.

Objectives:

To improve the HPV vaccine coverage in target populations. This training has been developed to enhance the HPV knowledge and communication skills of healthcare professionals.

Aimed at:

This course aims to enhance the HPV knowledge and communication skills of healthcare professionals.

Schedule:

From September 2nd to November 3rd

>Methodology:

The course consists of 20 hours of online content divided in 7 modules, which will be released weekly. It is designed to be completed at your own pace. Once released, the modules remain available until week 9 (two additional weeks are available for course completion), so that the student chooses the most convenient time for learning.

CME accreditation:

Each module has its own exam and certificate (CME accreditation is under review), so students can undertake only the modules they are interested in.
