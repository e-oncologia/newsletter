Raising awareness about HPV

Did you know? 📣

HPV causes six types of cancer,
but there’s one vaccine that can prevent them all.

 
 

The #PROTECTEUROPE project, funded under the #EU4Health programme, works to eliminate HPV-related cancers by equipping healthcare professionals with the tools and knowledge needed to increase vaccination rates and tackle misinformation.

The new animated video from this project explores the key facts about HPV and vaccination, highlighting the urgent need for action. Together, we can prevent thousands of cancer cases every year.
